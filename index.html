About Us

Nepalese Student Organization at MTU

The Nepalese Student Organization (NSO) at Michigan Technological University is a vibrant community dedicated to fostering cultural exchange, academic solidarity, and lifelong friendships among Nepali students and friends of Nepal.

We celebrate the richness of Nepali culture, support one another through the challenges of student life, and create a welcoming home away from home in the Upper Peninsula of Michigan.
